## What is Modes Manager?

The Complyance Modes Manager serves as your **authentication gateway** and **orchestrator** for global tax compliance. It simplifies complex e-invoicing requirements across multiple countries by providing a unified API interface that handles field mapping, document validation, and regulatory compliance automatically.

## How It Works

<Steps>
  <Step title="Create Integration">
    Start by creating an integration that defines your business requirements and target country
  </Step>
  <Step title="Upload Your Data">
    Provide your invoice or transaction data in any format - our AI will analyze the structure
  </Step>
  <Step title="AI Field Mapping">
    Our intelligent mapping engine automatically maps your fields to compliance requirements
  </Step>
  <Step title="Validate & Submit">
    Review mappings, validate against regulations, and generate compliant documents
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Popular Use Cases
